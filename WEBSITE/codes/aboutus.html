About Us

Ate G Sizzling Restaurant started in 2017. It began as an online order-only business until it grew and the owner established a restaurant. The establishment is located at 0001 J.M. PACIS STREET, SAUDI VILLAGE, LUNSAD, BINANGONAN, RIZAL. Their menu has SIZZLERS, ATE G'S BUDBOD, ATE G'S PANCIT, PLATTER, MIXED & MATCH, SNACKS/PULUTAN, DRINKS, and ATE G'S PANCIT.
